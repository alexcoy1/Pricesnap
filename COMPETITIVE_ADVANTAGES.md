How PriceSnap beats spreadsheets, generic quote tools, and heavy CRM quote modules for **Arctic Spa Durham** sales.

## vs. Excel Commission Sheets

| Spreadsheet pain | PriceSnap advantage |
|------------------|---------------------|
| Ctrl+F across 6 tabs (Classic, Core, Custom, AWP, Covana ×2) | **One merged 2026 list** — 327 SKUs, one-click load |

## 10 Unique Advantages PriceSnap Should Own

1. **Arctic Spa bundle intelligence** — spa match sets context for the correct Mylovac/Summit XL cover, not a random "cover" SKU.

2. **Commission-sheet native** — ingest the exact Excel format reps already have; no re-cataloging.

3. **No API key friction** — semantic matching runs in-browser via local embeddings; rule layer handles known patterns first.

4. **One-click 2026 price list** — new rep or fresh iPad is quoting in under 60 seconds.

5. **Match preview before send** — rep confirms lines before customer-facing quote (reduces embarrassment on live deals).

6. **Quick discount workflow** — 5–25% presets with **customer-visible savings summary** ("Your Savings: $X").

7. **Profit visibility on demand** — internal view for negotiation; customer never sees cost.

8. **Quick-bundle chips** — showroom phrases as one tap (Summit+Cover, Fox+Onzen, Covana 14ft).

9. **Field-ready output** — PDF for formal send, copy-summary for driveway text, duplicate for follow-up visits.

10. **Durham-local branding defaults** — Arctic Spa Durham company info, CAD terms, 30-day validity out of the box.
